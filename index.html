About Us

the secret of CoffeeAroma

CoffeeAroma, founded by Maria Hernandez in the early 1990s, has grown from a humble coffee spot to a beloved community fixture.
With its commitment to quality beans and welcoming ambiance, it's become a cherished hub for coffee enthusiasts seeking both a caffeine fix and a sense of belonging.

the secret behind CoffeeAroma's legendary coffee lies in a meticulously guarded recipe passed down through generations.
It starts with the finest beans sourced from remote corners of the globe, selected for their exquisite flavor profiles and carefully roasted to perfection.
